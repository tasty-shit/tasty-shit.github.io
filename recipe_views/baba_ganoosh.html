Baba Ganoosh

Cooked Augergines and garlic.

  • Cooked aubergine flesh (From the karni yarik recipe or from one whole Augergine)
  • 2 Garlic cloves
  • 1/2 tbps Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

  1. Blend the cooked aubergine and garlic or mash the cooked augergine, finely chop the garlic and mix well. Season with salt and pepper.

  2. Put in a frying pan with the olive oil and keep it over a low heat for 10 - 15 minutes. Stir it regularly.

  3. Serve with nice bread or rice or foor or whatever - it tastes good.
